Direct carbon capture system integrated with a bicycle. The system utilizes a solid phase absorber and a mechanical energy-driven system to capture and sequester CO2. The carbon capture system is retrofittable on a bicycle framework. The major components of the system consist of extended chain linkage, sprocket chain tensioner assembly, crank wheel, connecting rod, piston-compression cylinder, PU pneumatic pipe and the reaction chamber.
